About Us

FOCUS (Forum of Computer Upcoming Scholars) is a platform for all the CSE students in the University where you can express your greviances and get them solved. We always strive to provide the oppurtunities as much as we can in every aspect of academic related component. It empowers the capability of working individually and together in a team. FOCUS has been working since 18 years and is more effectively working since 2013. The word FOCUS refers to brilliance in all aspects, good decision-making and keen perceptional abilities of a student. The purpose of this forum is to empower the students with all the necessary skills required in this rapid growing and changing quality conscious globalized technological world. FOCUS helps them to develop as complete individuals and contribute to the nation development. FOCUS provides an ideal platform for the students to exchange their ideas and also to enlarge their knowledge. It also provides an opportunity to realize their potential and to have enthusiastic experience. Team work plays a vital role in today's technology world and also true, that knowledge can be enhanced by sharing. FOCUS provides such opportunities to all the students to work in teams and to realize the true potential teamwork and acquire knowledge. We also conduct National Level Techno Management Fests which will help you to know about the technology and all the new stuff by giving a break to the academic year for 2 to 3 days. Apart from technical, we do conduct non-technical events.
